Robogals' Rural Trip

📣 Rural Trip Applications Now Open 📣 Do you have some free time during the mid-year break? Are you interested in a free trip to the Wallaroo? Are you passionate about inspiring school students with STEM? Robogals Adelaide is running our annual rural trip, and are looking for volunteers to attend! We will be visiting schools in the Wallaroo region, and delivering Robogals workshops to school children in regional areas. This rural trip will take place from the 2nd-6th July. Workshops will run during the school day (9am-3pm), with free time for volunteers after this. We are looking for passionate and enthusiastic volunteers to apply for this opportunity, and welcome applications from any degree! Applications close Friday 26th of May at midnight.
